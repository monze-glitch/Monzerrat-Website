About Me

This domain is used to showcase the artwork made by the artist and illustrator, Monzerrat Gomez. With experience of different varieties of mediums, Monzerrat has made an effort to showcase her skill and knowledge to the test. She graduated from San Jose State University with a bachelors as she continues to demonstrate creativity throughout her work. Monzerrat foresees a professional future in art by curating painting, drawing, and illustrations. Her creative work has always been a mixture of illustration and digital art. Her art is mostly inspired by cartoons and animations that she simply grew up with and was her happy place to escape from reality. Monzerrat wishes to one day collab with other professionals and create a comic along with an animated TV series.
